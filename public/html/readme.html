New Functionalities

Please note that feature functionalities are still in progress.
  • The main app exists on THIS PAGE
  • If it is your first time accessing the page you will be taken to our index page and asked to register/login, all other links will direct you back to the main page
  • You may log in with the credentials mash@gmail.com/password, or create a new account
  • Only one game may be played at a time, starting a new game will delete old game process globally (for you and the other players)
  • Hide and Seek - A new game of Hide and Seek can be started in the Games page
  • Hide and Seek - After you select your players a seeker will be chosen.
  • Hide and Seek - A countdown will start giving all players 10 minutes to get to their positions.
  • Hide and Seek - Players may check-in early.
  • Hide and Seek - Players must check in every 3 minutes.
  • Hide and Seek - Accuracy is within 200m if using WiFi, we recommend GPS positioning.
  • Hide and Seek - If a seeker checks in within 100m of a hider, then they are given 5 points.
  • Hide and Seek - For every round (3 minutes) that a hider can evade a seeker, they are given 5 points.
  • Hide and Seek - The game is played for 5 rounds, and the player with the most points wins.

Idea

Find Me is a web application built for all wi-fi devices that facilitates meet-ups. Not only does it give users meeting place suggestions, it also shows real-time trajectory maps, calculates ETAs, gives access to a built-in chat, and incorporates fun geolocation games for users that are on-the-go. Find Me can also be used on the desktop to ensure that your loved ones get to where they need to go safely and with that grocery list on hand! Find Me is perfect for your next impromptu meeting.

New Technologies
